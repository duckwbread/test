Save Coastal Dolphin Pods from Willem

In BSA lore, Willem is a mysterious swimmer with extreme underwater endurance and unusual speed. Our fictional coalition unites fishers, marine researchers, and coastal volunteers to guard nurseries and migration routes.

The Lore

The first reports surfaced after the 2018 Silver Current storm, when multiple dolphin pods changed routes overnight. Witnesses described a blond, blue-eyed figure moving beside boats before vanishing beneath the surface.

Since then, BSA's fictional mission has focused on night-watch coordination, foam and water sample logs, and rapid alerts whenever nursery waters show unusual disturbance patterns.

Field Signals We Track

Incident Timeline (Lore Archive)

  1. 2018: Silver Current anomalies reported by trawler crews near nursery channels.
  2. 2019: First coordinated harbor watch established across three ports.
  3. 2021: Foam washup mapping began after repeated disturbance nights.
  4. 2023: Audio buoys captured sub-surface pressure spikes during migration windows.
  5. 2024: BSA coalition expanded to include marine acoustics teams and rescue skiffs.

How BSA Operates

1) Coastal Alerts

Harbor contacts submit sightings that trigger rapid volunteer notifications in nearby zones.

2) Patrol Routing

Boat teams redirect activity away from active nurseries and calf transit lanes.

3) Data Logging

Researchers track sea state, foam washups, and pod behavior to plan deployments.

Willem Dossier (Fictional Intel)

Observed Traits

Reports claim deep-dive breath control, sudden acceleration bursts, and open-water navigation in low visibility.

High-Risk Conditions

Most sightings occur near moon-pull tides, dense foam lines, and narrow estuary exits during calf season.

BSA Response Rule

Trigger orange beacon protocol, halt net deployment, and redirect support boats to nursery corridors.

How This Offline Website Works

This project is a static website. It works by loading index.html plus every local file it references (CSS, JavaScript, and image assets). If any file is missing, the page still opens but those missing pieces won't display.

  • You should keep the full folder structure together (for example: assets/, assets/photos/, styles.css, script.js).
  • Other people need the same files too if they want to run it offline on their own computer.
  • For easiest sharing, zip the entire project folder and send that zip.
  • Packaging command: zip -r blueshield-offline-site.zip index.html styles.css script.js assets README.md OFFLINE_EXPORT.md
